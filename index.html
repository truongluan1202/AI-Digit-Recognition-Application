AI DIGIT RECOGNITION TOOL

This application uses a Neural Network to recognize handwritten digits. It is trained on the MNIST dataset and achieves 95% accuracy in recognizing digits.

How to use:

  • Left-click to draw digits on the canvas.
  • Right-click or click on the "Erase" button to delete your drawing.
  • Click on the "Clear All" button or press "C" on your keyboard to clear the entire canvas.
